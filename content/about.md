I'm an amateur astronomer working through various Astronomical League observation programs. This site documents my observations, equipment, and astronomical journey.

## Astronomy Club

I'm a member of the [Barnard Astronomical Society of Chattanooga](https://www.barnardastronomy.org/), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to astronomy education and outreach in the Chattanooga, Tennessee community. I've served as Vice President since June 2024.

## Equipment

- **Orion Apex Maksutov** (102mm/1300mm f/12.7 Maksutov-Cassegrain)
