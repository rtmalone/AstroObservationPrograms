M30 - Capricornus Cluster

Jellyfish Cluster observed from Standing Stone State Park on an exceptional night with excellent transparency.

Object Type: Globular Cluster

Constellation: Capricornus

Right Ascension: 21h 40m 24s

Declination: -23° 11' 00"

Magnitude: 8.5

Size: 11.0'

My Observation Details
Object Name: M30 - Capricornus Cluster
Seeing: E
Transparency: 7
Lat/Lon: 36.47, -85.41
Instrument: 750mm/150mm
Power: 83.3x (9mm eyepiece)
Date: 2023-10-10
Time: 7:53 CST

Observation Notes

Visual Description

Typical nice bright center of stars, but the outer diffused stars are not as uniform. Looks like a dark shape eats into the bottom (in the eyepiece) of the cluster. Most surrounding stars are few and faint. Except for two: a gold star at 2:00 relative to M30 and a smaller blue star at 9:00.

Additional Notes

Used Telrad finder to locate this object.
