Observation Programs

My progress through various Astronomical League observation programs.

Double Star Program

Observe 100 double and multiple star systems to explore stellar companions. Successfully split pairs and record their separations, position angles, and colors.

Lunar I Program

Observe 100 specific lunar features including major craters, mountain ranges, seas (maria), and valleys. Learn to identify the Moon’s most prominent surface formations.

Lunar II Program

Advanced lunar observing requiring 100 additional challenging features. Focus on smaller craters, domes, rilles, and subtle formations. Lunar I completion required.

Messier Program

Observe all 110 Messier objects - the brightest deep-sky treasures including galaxies, nebulae, and star clusters. A classic introduction to deep-sky observing.
